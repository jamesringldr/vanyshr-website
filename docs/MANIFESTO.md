# Vanyshr Manifesto

*Founder copy — reference for About page, press, and future landing page usage.*

---

## Headline

**We built this out of anger.**

---

## Body

It started when I began getting hundreds of spam and scam calls — Medicare offers, fake real estate deals — 20 to 30 calls a day. Annoying, but ignorable.

Then it got personal. My mother was targeted by a phishing scam. The attackers had nothing but her phone number — but that was enough. They sent a fake bank alert, tricked her into entering her credentials on a look-alike page. Within hours, her account was emptied.

That was the moment I decided to understand exactly how our information gets out there — and how to take it back. I traced the sources of our exposure. I learned that data brokers and public records make it trivially easy for scammers to find and target us.

I started removing my own data. Then my friends'. Then my family's. Then, from pure hatred of these scammers, I decided to build something for the masses.

That's when I put a small team together and we built Vanyshr — a tool to fight back in the only way I could: by making their job harder.

We have no investors. No affiliations. We will never sell your data or send spammy emails. The second you ask for deletion, it's gone — no backups, no transfers. 100% deleted.

This isn't just software. It's my way of protecting the people I care about — and hopefully, you too.

---

*— James O., Founder, Vanyshr*
